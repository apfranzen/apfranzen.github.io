About

Goal-driven, dedicated and passionate software developer with a desire to work on meaningful projects. A builder at heart, I love tackling complex projects as a team. Through these experiences, I have gained the experience of breaking complex problems down into their smallest parts and creating modular solutions in a timely and efficient manner.

In my free-time, you can find me camping off of my motorcycle, cycling, fly fishing, shooting photography, woodworking and cooking.
